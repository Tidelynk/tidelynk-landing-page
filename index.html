How TideLynk Works

Tap. Match. Clean.

From the moment you open TideLynk to a cleaner at your door — fast, flexible, and verified every step of the way.

1

Tap

Open TideLynk and choose your booking type. LynkNow for on-demand matching, a specific date and time for Scheduled, or set up a Recurring clean for ongoing support.

2

Match

TideLynk matches you with verified local cleaners based on your location, time, and job scope. View real cleaner profiles, ratings, trust badges, and ETA — then choose.

3

Clean

Track your cleaner in real time, confirm job completion, and come home to a properly cleaned space. Rate your experience and set up a recurring schedule if you'd like.

LynkNow

Recurring

Set up weekly, fortnightly, or monthly cleaning after your first clean. Keep your preferred cleaner and automate your schedule.
