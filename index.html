How TideLynk Works

Tap. Match. Clean.

From the moment you open TideLynk to a cleaner at your door — fast, flexible, and verified every step of the way.

1

Tap

Open TideLynk and choose your booking type. LynkNow for on-demand matching, a specific date and time for Scheduled, or set up a Recurring clean for ongoing support. Enter your property details and job scope — done in under a minute.

2

Match

TideLynk matches you with verified local cleaners based on your location, time, and job scope. View real cleaner profiles, ratings, trust badges, and ETA — then choose the cleaner that suits you.

3

Clean

Track your cleaner in real time, confirm job completion, and come home to a properly cleaned space. Rate your experience and set up a recurring schedule if you'd like regular support.

Booking Modes

Three ways to book.

LynkNow

Our signature on-demand service. Get matched with a nearby verified cleaner in minutes. See real ETAs, live distance, and cleaner profiles before you confirm. Perfect when you need a clean fast.

Scheduled

Choose your preferred date and time. TideLynk matches you with cleaners available for your selected slot and job scope. Browse up to 5 verified cleaners and pick the right one for your home.

Recurring

Set up weekly, fortnightly, or monthly cleaning after your first clean. Keep your preferred cleaner, automate your schedule, and never think about booking again. Set it once and stay tidy automatically.

TideLynk is preparing for launch across Perth. Customers, independent cleaners, and cleaning companies can join the waitlist now to be among the first when the platform goes live.

TideLynk is being built for standard cleans, deep cleans, end of lease / bond cleans, scheduled cleans, and recurring home cleaning. LynkNow is TideLynk's signature on-demand matching service. Some service types may launch in stages.

LynkNow is TideLynk's signature on-demand service. It matches you with a nearby verified cleaner in real time — with live ETAs and distance information. Perfect for when you need a clean fast, without planning ahead.

With scheduled booking, you choose your preferred date and time. TideLynk then matches you with cleaners who are available for your selected slot and job scope. You can browse up to 5 verified cleaners and choose the one that suits you best.

After your first clean, TideLynk will prompt you to set up a recurring schedule — weekly, fortnightly, or monthly. You can keep your preferred cleaner and automate your booking. Set it once and your home stays tidy automatically.
